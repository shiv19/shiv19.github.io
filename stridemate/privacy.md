# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** November 25, 2024
**Last updated:** November 25, 2024

## Overview

Stridemate is a pace calculator app for runners. Your privacy is important to us, and we've designed this app with privacy as a core principle.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

Stridemate:
- Does not require an account or login
- Does not collect your name, email, or any personal information
- Does not track your location
- Does not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
- Does not use analytics or tracking services
- Does not display advertisements

## Data Storage

All app data (your saved calculations and preferences) is stored locally on your device only. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is stored in your browser's IndexedDB (for PWA) or device storage (for mobile apps)
- Is not synced to any server
- Is not backed up to the cloud by us

## Third-Party Services

Stridemate does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data. The app works entirely offline after initial download.

## External Links

The app contains links to:
- Ko-fi (for optional donations)
- This privacy policy page

When you tap these links, you will leave the app and those external sites have their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

Stridemate does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

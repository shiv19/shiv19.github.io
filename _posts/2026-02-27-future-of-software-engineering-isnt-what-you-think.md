AI is accelerating code generation, but the core engineering craft was never just typing syntax.
The enduring moat is judgment: framing the right problem, making system tradeoffs, and applying taste under uncertainty.
Great engineers will thrive by becoming stronger product thinkers who decide what should be built, not just how fast it can be built.

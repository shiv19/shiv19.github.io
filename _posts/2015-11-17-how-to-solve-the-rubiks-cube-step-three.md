Orientate the last layer!

<p><img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/Rubiks-Step-3-1024x307.png" alt="Rubik's Step 3" width="100%" height="307" /></p>
<p>This post will guide you through the Step Three in How to Solve the Rubiks Cube.
    If you are here after reading the Step Two then please read along. Else <a href="{{ site.baseurl }}/how-to-solve-the-rubiks-cube/">CLICK HERE</a> to goto Step One. Assuming you mastered Step Two, you should now be able to solve the First Layer of the cube. Now your cube should look something like this:<br/> <img class="aligncenter wp-image-134 size-full" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/After-Step-Two.png" alt="After Step Two" width="223" height="232" /><br/> Now let's move on to the next step on How to Solve the Rubiks Cube.</p>
<h1>STEP 3: Orientation of Last Layer</h1>
<p>Your goal in this step is to make all the pieces on the top layer Yellow. So after this step, your cube should look something like this. <br/><img class="aligncenter wp-image-136 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/Goal2-283x300.png" alt="Goal" width="283" height="300" /><br/> I've broken down this step into 2 phases.</p>
<h4>Phase 1: The Yellow Cross</h4>
<p><br/><img class="aligncenter wp-image-137 size-full" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/Goal_intermediate.png" alt="The Yellow Cross" width="291" height="264" /><br/> If you already have a yellow cross on your cube then you can skip this phase and move to phase 2. Else hold your cube such that it matches the yellow pattern on the top (U) face of your cube to one of the yellow patterns shown on the cubes below. Then follow the corresponding sequence.</p>
<p><strong>State 1: The Dot Case</strong> <br/><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-138" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/Dot-Case.png" alt="Dot Case" width="152" height="170" /><br/> If your cube matches this case then apply the Algorithm Below and then match the result to state 2 or 3, hold the cube to match that state and do that sequence:</p><br/><img class="wp-image-139" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/For-L-Case-300x79.png" alt="F U R Ui Ri Fi" width="500" height="132" /><br/> F U R Ui Ri Fi<br/>
<p><strong>State 2: The L Case</strong> <br/><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-140" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/L-Case.png" alt="L Case" width="151" height="163" /><br/> If your cube matches this case then apply the Algorithm Below:</p><br/><img class="wp-image-139" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/For-L-Case-300x79.png" alt="F U R Ui Ri Fi" width="500" height="132" /><br/> F U R Ui Ri Fi<br/>
<p><strong>State 3: The Straight Line Case</strong> <br/><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-141" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/Straight-Line-Case.png" alt="Straight Line Case" width="153" height="168" /><br/> If your cube matches this case then apply the Algorithm Below:</p><br/><img class="wp-image-142" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/For-Straight-Line-Case-300x77.png" alt="F R U Ri Ui Fi" width="500" height="129" /><br/> F R U Ri Ui Fi<br/>
<h4>Phase 2: The Yellow Top</h4>
<p><br/><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-143" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/for-phase-2.png" alt="Phase 2" width="285" height="258" /><br/> If you already have a yellow top on your cube then you can skip this phase and move to the next Step! Else hold your cube such that it matches the yellow pattern on the top (U) face of your cube to one of the yellow patterns shown on the cubes below. Then use this same algorithm for each state. (Make sure that Yellow is still your top face and White is at the bottom).</p><br/><img class="wp-image-144" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/phase-2-algo-300x53.png" alt="R U Ri U R U U Ri" width="500" height="88" /><br/> R U Ri U R U U Ri<br/>
<p><strong>State 1: </strong>If <strong>No Corner </strong>Cubes are Yellow on the top (U) face, you must have Yellow <b>Left </b>Corner on the Left face as shown below and do the algorithm above. <br/><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-145" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/State-1-phase-2.png" alt="State 1 phase 2" width="220" height="153" /><br/> <strong>State 2: </strong>If <strong>One Corner </strong>Cube is Yellow, match your top (U) face as shown and do the algorithm above. <br/><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-146" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/State-2-phase-2.png" alt="State 2 phase 2" width="167" height="165" /><br/> <strong>State 3: </strong>If Any <strong>Two Corner </strong>Cubes are Yellow don't worry about their position. You <strong>MUST</strong> match the front (F) face with a Yellow Left Corner as shown. <br/><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-147" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/rb3/State-3-phase-2.png" alt="State 3 phase 2" width="195" height="185" /><br/>   If you have carefully followed the above steps, you should be able to make the Yellow top. If you have done so, then you are ready to move to the next step.</p>

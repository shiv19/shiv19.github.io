Easiest way to make Maggi in a Bowl!

![Post Header Image]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/Headder-Image2-1024x267.png "14 Windows Key Shortcuts That You Should Know")

<h1>Welcome to SHIV's ULTIMATE GUIDE TO MAKE BOWL Maggi!</h1>
<p>My friends have always been asking me what's the secret recipe behind tasty bowl Maggi that I make. So, on this fine day, I decided to share my recipe for making Maggi in a bowl!</p>
<h2>Disclaimer:</h2>
<p>You don't have to follow some of the steps shown in this article, as they were made purely for entertainment.</p>
<h2>Things you'll need:</h2>
<ul>
   <li>Maggi pack (Worth â‚¹12 at the time of writing this article).</li>
   <li>A bowl.</li>
   <li>A spoon/fork.</li>
   <li>Kettle to boil the water.</li>
   <li>Del Monte Tomato Ketchup (not mandatory).</li>
</ul>
<h2>Step 1:</h2>
<p>Wash the bowl, wash the spoon. Keep the spoon in the bowl. Keep the bowl on the table.<br /> It should look like this.</p>

<img class="wp-image-65 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-1-e1450506586793-300x286.jpg" alt="step 1" width="300" height="286" /> 

It should look like this.

<h2>Step 2:</h2>
<p>Take out your pack of Maggi. </p>

<img class="wp-image-66 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-2-e1450507319769-273x300.jpg" alt="From a place like this" width="273" height="300" />

From a place like this

<h2>Step 3:</h2>
<p>Break the Maggi pack into half vertically and horizontally before opening the pack.</p>

<img class="wp-image-67 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-3-e1450507362182-265x300.jpg" alt="Like this." width="265" height="300" />

Like this.

<img class="wp-image-68 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-3_2-e1450507382586-241x300.jpg" alt="And this." width="241" height="300" />

And this.

<h2>Step 4:</h2>
<p>Open the pack. Keep the masala aside. And put the Maggi in the bowl.</p>

<img class="aligncenter wp-image-69 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-4-e1450507429325-293x300.jpg" alt="step 4" width="293" height="300" />

<p>Make sure all the raw noodle cakes are of single layer.</p>

<img class="wp-image-70 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-4_2-e1450507452614-232x300.jpg" alt="Like this" width="232" height="300" />

Like this

<h2>Step 5:</h2>
<p>Pour the masala into the bowl.</p>
<p>Like this</p>

<img class="wp-image-71 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-5-e1450507483417-269x300.jpg" alt="Bowl raw maggie ready! (half way there)" width="269" height="300" />

Bowl raw Maggi ready! (half way there)

<h2>Step 6:</h2>
<p>Take out your kettle (wash it) and put some fresh drinkable water into it.<br /> About 300 to 400 ml of water is fine.. (400ml for extra soupy Maggi and 300ml for soupy Maggi)<br /> If you don't want it soupy.. I'll tell you what to do later.</p>

<img class="aligncenter wp-image-72 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-6-e1450507509533-219x300.jpg" alt="step 6" width="219" height="300" />

<h2>Step 7:</h2>
<p>Plug the kettle and let the water boil.</p>

<img class="wp-image-73 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-7-e1450507542887-203x300.jpg" alt="Make sure your face looks like this when you wait for it to boil" width="203" height="300" />

Make sure your face looks like this when you wait for it to boil

<img class="wp-image-74 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-7_2-e1450507592616-255x300.jpg" alt="Once it starts boiling leave it for 10 second" width="255" height="300" />

Once it starts boiling leave it for 10 second

<img class="wp-image-75 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-7_3-e1450507619216-216x300.jpg" alt="Pour all water" width="216" height="300" />

Pour all water

<h2>Step 8:</h2>
<p>Mix masala</p>
<img class="wp-image-76 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-8-e1450507645941-300x289.jpg" alt="Turn spoon in circular way for 10 seconds" width="300" height="289" />

Turn spoon in circular way for 10 seconds
<p>Make sure all noodles are immersed in water</p>

<h2>Step 9: </h2>

<img class="wp-image-77 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-9-e1450507670445-300x265.jpg" alt="keep a plate or a book on it" width="300" height="265" />

Keep a plate or a book on it

<h2>Step 10:</h2>
<p>Wait for about 3 to 5 minutes.</p>

<img class="wp-image-82 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-10-e1450507696550-200x300.jpg" alt="Make sure your face looks like this while you wait this time." width="200" height="300" />

Make sure your face looks like this while you wait this time.
<p>It's always good to make sure everything is immersed when the time is half way through. <br /> (at about 1.5 minutes)</p>
<p>Use this time to keep the kettle back at where it was. <br /> You don't have to wash it.. (that's the best part of bowl Maggi)</p>

<h2>Step 11:</h2>
<p>Time is up! <br /> Take off the plate and look at the Maggi</p>

<img class="wp-image-78 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-11-e1450507725338-208x300.jpg" alt="Like this" width="208" height="300" />

Like this

<img class="aligncenter wp-image-83 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-11_2-e1450507750612-252x300.jpg" alt="maggie" width="252" height="300" />

<h2>Step 12:</h2>
<p>Suggested garnishing. <br /> I'm going to use <strong>Del Monte Tomato Ketchup</strong>.</p>
<img class="aligncenter wp-image-79 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-12-e1450507774480-255x300.jpg" alt="step 12" width="255" height="300" />

<img class="aligncenter wp-image-80 size-medium" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/step-12_2-e1450507801651-255x300.jpg" alt="step 12_2" width="255" height="300" />

<p>Now mix the sauce by rotating spoon in circular fashion for 10sec<br /> This will turn your average Maggi soup into tomato soup!<br /> Keep the plate aside</p>
<p><strong>BOWL Maggi IS READY!</strong></p>

<img class="size-medium wp-image-81" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/bmaggi/conclusion-169x300.jpg" alt="Eat it like this with  maggie tomato soup ;)" width="169" height="300" />

Eat it like this with  Maggi tomato soup ;)
<p><strong>Note:</strong><br /> <strong> If you don't like soupy Maggi you'll have to drain the water.</strong></p>
<h3>Conclusion:<br /> If you want to save yourself from washing the kettle or making it a mess. <br /> Then bowl Maggi is perfect for you.</h3>
